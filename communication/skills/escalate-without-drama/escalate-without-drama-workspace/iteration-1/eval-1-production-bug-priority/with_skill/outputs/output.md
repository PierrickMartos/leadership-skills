# Escalation Draft — Production Bug Priority

[Your name]

**Notes**
- This draft keeps Thomas in a neutral light — framing him as someone who may simply be stretched, not unresponsive or dropping the ball. The concern stays squarely on the business impact, not on anyone's behavior.
- The closing ask is double-barreled: priority reconsideration + ETA. This gives Priya two concrete things to react to and makes it easy for her to act without needing to diagnose the whole situation herself.
- The line about PM-side prep ("is there anything I should be getting ahead of") signals good faith and partnership — you're not just escalating and walking away, you're asking how you can help. This tends to land well with VPs.
- If your relationship with Priya is more informal or you communicate frequently over Slack, you can trim this significantly — the core message is: 12% onboarding block, 24 hours, P2 feels low given impact, need ETA. The longer version above is appropriate for a slightly more formal or cross-functional escalation dynamic.
