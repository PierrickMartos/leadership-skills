# Data Platform Modernization — Steering Committee Approval Request

## Recommendation

Migrate our data platform to Snowflake. **Requesting approval to proceed to Phase 1.**

## Financial Case

| | 3-Year Cost |
|---|---|
| Current platform | $3.4M |
| Snowflake (post-migration) | $2.1M |
| **Net savings** | **$1.3M (38%)** |

## What We Did to Get Here

Since January, the team completed a full vendor evaluation (Snowflake, Databricks, BigQuery across 12 criteria), 14 stakeholder interviews across Engineering, Analytics, Marketing, and Finance, and a comprehensive technical assessment. Snowflake ranked highest on cost, latency, ecosystem fit, and migration complexity.

## Migration Plan

18-month rollout across 4 phases. Phase 1 — foundation setup and pilot migration of 3 non-critical datasets — is scoped at 6 weeks and can begin immediately upon approval.

## Key Risks

1. **Pipeline re-engineering complexity** — mitigation plan in place; phased approach limits blast radius
2. **Analyst productivity during transition** — training and parallel-run period built into Phase 1
3. **Vendor lock-in** — acceptable given cost delta and Snowflake's open standards support

## Decision Needed

Approve Phase 1 to start immediately. No budget commitment beyond Phase 1 is required at this stage.
